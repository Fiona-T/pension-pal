What is PensionPal?

PensionPal is an online portal where you can record details of your employments along with their associated occupational pension scheme. You record the details that will be required when you come to draw down the pension benefits at retirement age.

Why Use PensionPal?

  • All your pension details are in one place, linked to the relevant employment
  • You won’t forget about any of your pension plans
  • Easier to plan for the future
  • Simpler to record the relevant details ‘as you go’ e.g. when you leave an employment, while they are fresh in your mind and the documentation is to hand, rather than trying to pull the details together years later

How to Use PensionPal?

It couldn’t be easier! Sign Up for an account, then get started adding your Employments and associated Pensions.

  • Add the Employment first, from the My Jobs page, Add Employment
  • You can add an Employment without attaching a Pension to it, since not all jobs have a pension attached
  • Next, to attach a Pension to the Employment, go to the My Pensions page and Add Pension

Begin by adding your previous employments and pensions. This may take a bit of time, (which just reinforces the benefit of recording the details as you go!) but recording future pensions will be much easier. We recommend recording Employment and Pension details every time you change jobs in order to get full benefit out of the tool.
